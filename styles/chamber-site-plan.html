Duque de Caxias Chamber of Commerce - Site Plan

1 - Site Name

Name: "Duque de Caxias Chamber of Commerce"

Justification: This name indicates the geographical location, the purpose of the site and the services that will be offered in Duque de Caxias.

2 - Site Purpose

Purpose: The Duque de Caxias Chamber of Commerce is a hub for exploring valuable resources such as guides, workshops, and industry reports to support business growth and development and stay informed about economic trends. It also allows access to a comprehensive listing of local businesses, including contact information and descriptions, to facilitate networking and collaboration.

3 - Scenarios

  • "What are the upcoming networking events hosted by the Chamber?"
  • "What campaigns were conducted to help women-led businesses grow in the region in the last five years?"
  • "What was the latest research carried out by the chamber in the region? Where can I get the results"
  • "How can I suggest new surveys to the chamber?"

4 - Color Schema

Primary Color (Midnight Blue #1D4D72): Used for headers, important links, and other key elements to denote professionalism and trust. This deep shade of blue establishes a strong presence and anchors the site’s design.

Accent Color (Goldenrod #daa520): Used for buttons, highlighted text, and active elements to draw attention and create a visually appealing contrast. Goldenrod adds a touch of luxury and importance to interactive components.

Background Color (Light Gray #F0F0F0): Applied to most of the website’s background, this color provides a clean and unobtrusive backdrop that enhances readability and reduces visual strain.

Text Color (Eerie Black #181818): Used for the main body text, this near-black shade ensures excellent readability against lighter backgrounds and contributes to a minimalist and elegant aesthetic.

Secondary Text Color (White #FFFFFF): Used for text over areas with the primary Midnight Blue background or other dark sections. This color choice ensures high contrast and readability in varied visual contexts.

5 - Typography

Primary Font: 'Lato' for headings to add modern design.

Secondary Font: 'Noto Serif' for body text due to its high readability and good compatibility.
