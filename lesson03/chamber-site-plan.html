Springfield Chamber of Commerce - Site Plan

1 - Site Name

Name: "Springfield Chamber of Commerce"

Justification: This name indicates the geographical location, the purpose of the site and the services that will be offered in Springfield.

2 - Site Purpose

Purpose: The Springfield Chamber of Commerce is a hub for exploring valuable resources such as guides, workshops, and industry reports to support business growth and development and stay informed about economic trends. It also allows access to a comprehensive listing of local businesses, including contact information and descriptions, to facilitate networking and collaboration.

3 - Scenarios

  • "What are the upcoming networking events hosted by the Chamber?"
  • "What campaigns were conducted to help women-led businesses grow in the region in the last five years?"
  • "What was the latest research carried out by the chamber in the region? Where can I get the results"
  • "How can I suggest new surveys to the chamber?"

4 - Color Schema

Primary Color (Picton Blue #00A6ED): Used for headers, important links, and other key elements to denote professionalism and trust. Blue is a color of loyalty, strenght, wisdom and trust in the site design.

Accent Color (Apple green #7FB800): Used for buttons, highlighted text, and active elements to draw attention and create a visually appealing contrast. Apple green is a color that is considered a slightly darker yellowish-green, which conveys a sense of reliability and groundedness.

Background Color (Selective yellow #FFB400): Selective Yellow is a warm color that it has high lightness and high saturation.

Text Color (Berkeley blue #OD2C54): Used for the main body text, this near-black shade ensures excellent readability against lighter backgrounds and contributes to a minimalist and elegant aesthetic.

Secondary Text Color (Tangelo #F6511D): Used for text over areas with the primary Berkeley blue background or other dark sections. This color choice ensures high contrast and readability in varied visual contexts.

5 - Typography

Primary Font: 'Josefim Slab' for headings to add modern design.

Secondary Font: 'Alegreya' for body text due to its high readability and good compatibility.
