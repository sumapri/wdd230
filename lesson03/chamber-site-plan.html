Site Name

Missouri Chamber of Commerce

Purpose

The Springfield Area Chamber of Commerce top priority is bringing visitors to town and through the doors of your business. We take pride in serving as the voice of nearly 1,000 members, marketing to millions of visitors, and growing the region through economic development. Schedule a meeting today to begin sharing your story and to help shape the future of our community. Also,the purpose of the site and the services that will be offered in Springfield is help the community around this area to have the best experience and service their never imagined before.

Scenarios

  • We advocate for specific industries or regions and we can provide training and educational opportunities for members.
  • "What are the upcoming networking events hosted by the Chamber?"
  • "What campaigns were conducted to help women-led businesses grow in the region in the last five years?"
  • "What was the latest research carried out by the chamber in the region? Where can I get the results"
  • "How can I suggest new surveys to the chamber?"

The amber droplet hung from the branch, reaching fullness and ready to drop. It waited. While many of the other droplets were satisfied to form as big as they could and release, this droplet had other plans. It wanted to be part of history. So it waited for the perfect specimen to fly by to trap and capture that it hoped would eventually be discovered hundreds of years in the future.
